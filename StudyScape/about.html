What is StudyScape?

StudyScape is a quiet-zone map created for students at The University of Texas at Dallas. It helps you quickly find safe, comfortable, and aesthetic places to study – whether that’s a hidden corner in McDermott or a cozy café nearby.

How it works

Browse

Start on the home page and scroll through UTD and off-campus study spot cards.

Filter

Use simple filters like Outdoor, With Outlet, Coffee Nearby, and Silent Zone.

Contribute

Add your own favorite spots so future Comets can study there too.

How to use StudyScape

  • Choose whether you want an on-campus UTD spot or a nearby café.
  • Read the amenities and comments so you know what to expect.
  • Use the Directions and Maps buttons on the spot details page.
  • Submit new spots whenever you discover a good study vibe.

Contact / Support
