JSOM Atrium High Tables

★★★☆☆ · 3.9 / 5 · 27 ratings

Tall study tables in the JSOM atrium with large windows and a view of the courtyard. Bright, open, and social – best if you don’t need silence.

Amenities

  • High tables with bar-stool style chairs
  • Outlets near some sections of the wall
  • Lots of natural light from large windows
  • Medium noise level from conversations and footsteps

Comments

“Nice for team meetings when you want to feel awake and around people. Not great if you’re trying to cram in total silence.”
